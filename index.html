Can you find yourself described in this event?

Competitor:

This is the person who is ready to learn how to software engineer. Who wants to win the cash prize of $1500, $1000, or $500. Who is ready to see why software engineering is so valuable...

Affiliate:

This is the person who won't write any code, but knows people or someone who would be a great candidate for the competition. This is person receives commission for each person that signs up through them.

Sponsor:

This is the person who won't write any code, but wants to give Hall County teenagers a chance to do something special. They want to help a student who can't afford to pay for the registration. You want to see the face of the person you impacted.

Vounteer:

This person wants to help make a difference. You have the hands to work. You are easy to work with and want to be involved. You are ready to give your most precious commodity: time.
